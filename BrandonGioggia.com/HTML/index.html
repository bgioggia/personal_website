Boolean Network Grid

A boolean network is a made up of a bunch of nodes that can either be on or off. They are interesting because they usually have an associated set of rules that will determine when nodes turn on or off over time. These are useful in biology for modeling gene regulatory networks, but they also just make really cool patterns. I made a grid where you can adjust the rules that turns each cell on or off.
Playing with this one can eat up more time than you realize so be careful and have fun!

Amazon Alexa Tic Tac Toe

A skill for Amazon Alexa to keep track of your tic tac toe game in case you don't have any paper, but you do have your Amazon Echo. I made this because I thought the idea was funny, but there are still about 5-10 active users each week according to the dev console metrics. Whoever those 5-10 people are, I hope they're having fun!
