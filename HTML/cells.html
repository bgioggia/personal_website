Cells
Brandon Gioggia
Directions: Click in the box at the center of the screen to activate or deactivate cells.

The 'off' and 'on' buttons on the right will activate or deactivate cells when they have the corresponding number of activated neighbors.

If both buttons for one number (except for zero!) are pressed, it will be as though neither are, until one is turned off.

Press the 'Start' botton at the top right to begin the simulation!

If you want to clear the board, just activate all of the off buttons! (or refresh the page)
